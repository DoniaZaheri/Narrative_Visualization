Fuel Efficiency for Different Vehicle Brands

Understanding the mileage per galon use for different types of vehicles is an essential factor when deciding on buying a new vehilce or determining fuel regulations for different types of vehicles, especially with the drastic effects of climate change and increasing number of vehicles in the world. There are many factors that can affect the mileage of a vehicle (Link). This interactive visualization explores 2017 cars highway and city MPGs data, with respect to fuel type and the make of the vehicle.

The plot compares different brands in regards to their average city and highway MPG.
